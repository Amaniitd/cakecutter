About CakeCutters

CakeCutters is a platform developed by Aman Kumar, a solo developer, with the goal of facilitating fair resource division among individuals. The cake cutting algorithms implemented in this platform provide solutions to common sharing scenarios, such as dividing rent in a shared flat or splitting costs in a group setting.
